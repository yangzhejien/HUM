Review Policy

审核规则

Our Policy

We only reject content containing illegal acts, abuse, discrimination, or hate speech. All other viewpoints are permitted.

What We Reject

The following types of content will be rejected:

  • Illegal content
  • Abuse & harassment
  • Discrimination
  • Hate speech
  • Provocative content aimed at causing conflict

What We Welcome

Beyond the above restrictions, we welcome all viewpoints, perspectives, and opinions:

  • ✓ Personal essays and opinions
  • ✓ Political commentary
  • ✓ Philosophical discussions
  • ✓ Religious perspectives
  • ✓ Scientific theories
  • ✓ Creative writing
  • ✓ Academic research

Review Process

Initial Review: All submissions are acknowledged within 3-5 business days.

Compliance Check: We verify content does not contain illegal acts, abuse, discrimination, or hate speech.

Publication: Once cleared, articles are published promptly.

"The purpose of our review policy is not to censor ideas, but to maintain a civilized platform for discourse. Beyond this, all voices are welcome."
