Journal Introduction

Two Sections, One Vision

This platform is a non-profit personal exchange network journal, not an officially published journal.

HUM-Public 大众刊

The 大众刊 (Public Journal) is our open platform for expression without barriers. We believe that every voice matters and that ideas should flow freely.

Scope

  • Personal essays and reflections
  • Opinion pieces and commentary
  • Creative writing and prose
  • Thought experiments and philosophical musings
  • Social observations and cultural analysis
  • Interdisciplinary explorations

Key Features

  • No submission barriers
  • Focus on expression and thought flow
  • All viewpoints welcome (within legal limits)
  • Quick initial review process

HUM-Academic 专业刊

The 专业刊 (Academic Journal) is our platform for scholarly work with emphasis on logic, rigor, and academic standards. We are building a foundation for future formal development.

Scope

  • Research papers and empirical studies
  • Theoretical frameworks and analyses
  • Literature reviews and meta-analyses
  • Case studies and applied research
  • Academic commentaries and critiques
  • Methodological discussions

Key Features

  • Emphasis on logical structure
  • Citation and reference requirements
  • Peer review process
  • Academic formatting standards

A Clear Distinction

Aspect HUM-Public HUM-Academic
Barrier None Moderate
Format Flexible Structured
Citations Optional Required
Review Basic compliance Peer review
Focus Expression Rigor
