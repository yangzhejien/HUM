Review Rules

What We Review / 我们审核什么

Our Core Principle

We only review for content that violates the law, contains abuse, discrimination, or provocation. All other viewpoints are allowed.

What We Review / 我们审核的内容

Our review process is intentionally minimal. We believe in the free exchange of ideas and welcome diverse perspectives. However, to maintain a safe and legal environment, we do NOT publish content that:

  • Violates Laws / 违法
  • Contains Abuse / 辱骂
  • Promotes Discrimination / 歧视
  • Incites Conflict / 引战

Beyond these four categories, all viewpoints, opinions, and perspectives are welcome. We do not judge the quality or acceptability of ideas — only their compliance with basic legal and ethical standards.

Detailed Review Standards / 详细审核标准

01

Legal Compliance

Content must not violate any applicable laws. This includes but is not limited to: defamation, copyright infringement, national security violations, or any illegal activities.

02

No Abusive Language

Content must not contain personal attacks, hate speech, or derogatory remarks directed at individuals or groups. Criticism of ideas, policies, or actions is acceptable; personal vilification is not.

03

No Discrimination

Content must not promote discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or any other protected characteristic.

04

No Provocation

Content must not be designed to inflame tensions, provoke conflict, or incite violence. Strong opinions and debates are welcome; intentional provocation is not.

Review Process / 审核流程

All submissions undergo an initial review by our Initial Review Editor, Wither. The process is as follows:

  1. Submission received and acknowledged within 3-5 business days
  2. Initial compliance check (5-7 days for HUM-Public, 14-30 days for HUM-Academic)
  3. Decision notification sent to author
  4. If accepted, publication scheduled
